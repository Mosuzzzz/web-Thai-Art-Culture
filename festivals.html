Vibrant Festivals

Celebrations of Life and Tradition

Loy Krathong

This is hands down one of the most magical nights in Thailand! During the full moon of the 12th lunar month, everyone heads to rivers and lakes to float these gorgeous lotus-shaped baskets with candles. It's a way to thank the water goddess and let go of negativity. The whole scene is just mesmerizing!

Songkran

Thai New Year = biggest water fight ever! Seriously, Songkran (April 13th) is SO much fun. The water throwing symbolizes washing away bad luck, but honestly it just turns into this massive street party. Don't forget to visit your family and pay respects to elders too - that's the traditional part!

Yi Peng

If Loy Krathong is magical, Yi Peng in Chiang Mai is absolutely surreal. Imagine thousands of glowing lanterns floating up into the night sky all at once - it's like something out of a dream! Happens around the same time as Loy Krathong. Put this on your bucket list, trust me.
