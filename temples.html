Majestic Temples

Sacred Architecture of Thailand

Wat Phra Kaew

If you ever visit Bangkok, you absolutely have to see Wat Phra Kaew - it's honestly breathtaking. This temple houses the famous Emerald Buddha and sits right inside the Grand Palace complex. The whole place just radiates this incredible spiritual energy.


What really gets me is the detail in everything - the golden chedis, the colorful mosaics, and these amazing mythical guardian statues. It's like stepping into another world!

Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn)

Wat Arun is probably one of the most photographed temples in Bangkok, and for good reason. It's located on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River, and the name comes from Aruna, the Hindu god associated with the dawn.


The central tower is covered in these beautiful pieces of colorful porcelain and seashells - it looks absolutely stunning when the sun hits it. Definitely try to catch it at sunset if you can!
