Thai Sports

Strength, Agility, and Tradition

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, or "The Art of Eight Limbs," is Thailand's national sport and martial art. It is characterized by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins. Known for its effectiveness and power, Muay Thai is practiced worldwide for fitness, self-defense, and competition.

Sepak Takraw

Sepak Takraw is a sport native to Southeast Asia, resembling volleyball but played with a rattan ball and using only the feet, knees, chest, and head. It requires immense agility and acrobatic skills, with players often performing mid-air flips to spike the ball.
