Exquisite Arts

Masterpieces of Thai Craftsmanship

Benjarong

Benjarong is a traditional form of Thai porcelain. The name literally means "five colors." The hand-painted pieces are known for their incredibly intricate patterns and the use of gold.

Thai Amulets

Thai Buddha amulets are sacred items believed to provide protection and good luck. They are often made from clay, metal, or powder and blessed by monks.

Traditional Painting

Thai traditional painting is primarily confined to mural paintings inside Buddhist temples and palaces, characterized by a lack of perspective and the use of flat colors.
